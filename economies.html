Kids Need School
for Nutritious Food.

We know kids who eat healthy do better academically, physically and emotionally.
Currently only 2% of school meal funds are spent on NC-grown produce.
By providing state funding for more North Carolina grown foods in schools, we could make a big difference for our school children, farmers AND our local economies.  

Feed NC kids with food from NC farmers.

Children and adolescents consume up to half of their daily calories at school
Let’s work together and make sure our NC farmers are providing more of the food our kids eat every day.
Every child.  Every school.  Every day.
